California Backyard Birds

There are many different type of California Backyard Birds. Here are some different birds that you can find sometimes find in your very own backyard.
For more information on birds, try going to Project FeederWatch.
I'M CALIFORNIA TOWHEE GLAMOUR BIRD

I may be prompted by a tireless knocking at your window or car mirror
I'M A HOUSE FINCH BIRD

I build my nest in cavities,building,hanging plants,and other cup-shaped outdoor decorations
I'M NORTHERN MOCKINGBIRD

My mimcking ability,as reflected by name means'many-tongued mimic.
I'M A SONG SPARROW BIRD

I use melodious and faily complex song to declare ownership of territory and to attract females
I'M A WESTERN MEADOWLARK

My buoyant,flutelike melody can brighten anyone's day
I'M A WESTERN SRUB JAYBIRD

I am a fixture of dry shrublands,oak woodiands,and conspicuous visitors to backyards.

California Backyard Birds are beautiful creatures!
